Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce concepts used to define how organizations use networks and cloud computing in information systems.

After completing this module you will be able to:

  1.  Identify and briefly describe three network topologies and four different network types, including the uses and limitations of each
  2.  Identify and briefly discuss several types of both guided and wireless communications
  3.  Identify several network hardware devices and define their functions
  4.  Briefly describe how the Internet and the Web work, including various methods for connecting to the Internet
  5.  Outline the process and tools used in developing Web content and applications
  6.  List and briefly describe several Internet and Web applications
  7.  Explain how intranets and extranets use Internet technologies, and describe how the two differ
  8.  Define what is meant by the Internet of Things (IoT), and explain how it works
  9.  Identify and briefly discuss several practical applications of the Internet of Things (IoT)
  10.  Categorize and summarize several potential issues and barriers associated with the expansion of the Internet of Things (IoT)
  11.  Discuss how cloud computing can increase the speed and reduce the costs of new product and service launches
  12.  Summarize three common problems organizations encounter in moving to the cloud
  13.  Discuss the pros and cons of private and hybrid cloud computing compared to public cloud computing
