Learning Objectives

Define the Field of Management Information Systems (MIS) and its Role in Organizations

  • Management Information Systems (MIS) is the field that focuses on how people, technology, and organizations use information to improve decision-making, enhance efficiency, and gain competitive advantage.
  • MIS professionals design, implement, and manage systems that help businesses collect, process, store, and use data to solve problems and meet strategic goals.
  • In organizations, MIS plays a critical role in supporting operations, facilitating communication, and enabling innovation.
  • Whether it's tracking inventory, analyzing sales trends, or streamlining customer service, MIS bridges the gap between business needs and technology solutions, helping all departments-from marketing and finance to Human Resources-work smarter and more effectively.

Case Overview

SmartBean Coffee

SmartBean Coffee is a rapidly growing coffee chain that started as a college-town favorite and now operates over 80 stores nationwide.
While their coffee quality is top-notch, they are facing operational challenges as they scale.
The founder, an entrepreneur with a marketing background, realizes that spreadsheets and email chains are no longer sufficient to handle inventory, scheduling, marketing analytics, or customer loyalty.

To move forward, she's hired a junior business analyst-you-to help her understand what Management Information Systems (MIS) is, and why it might be the foundation for making better decisions, improving communication, and streamlining operations.
