Case Overview

SmartBean Coffee and Big Data

SmartBean Coffee has recently implemented loyalty apps and online ordering across all of its locations.
As a result, it is now collecting vast amounts of data-purchase history, app usage, customer preferences, time-of-day trends, and more.
The leadership team is interested in using this data to drive better decisions, such as optimizing store hours, targeting promotions, and improving product offerings.
You have been asked to explain how big data is managed and used in modern organizations, and how it supports business intelligence (BI) and data-driven decision making.
