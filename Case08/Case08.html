Case Overview

SmartBean Coffee Evaluates Enterprise Systems

SmartBean Coffee has grown beyond 80 locations and now faces challenges in coordinating sales, inventory, purchasing, and accounting across stores.
The company's current systems consist of isolated applications and spreadsheets managed independently at each location, leading to duplication, delays, and errors.
Leadership is considering adopting an integrated enterprise resource planning (ERP) system, with optional customer relationship management (CRM) and product lifecycle management (PLM) modules to support future growth.
You have been asked to research transaction processing systems (TPS), evaluate different enterprise system options, and explore the pros and cons of hosted (cloud-based) models-especially for a growing small-to-medium-sized enterprise (SME) like SmartBean.
