Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce structures and management concepts used to define how organizations use information systems.

After completing this module you will be able to:

  1. Describe change models that increases the likelihood of successfully introducing a new information system in an organization .
  2. Explain reengineering and continuous improvement .
  3. Show how information systems support and work within the automated portions of an organizational process .
  4. Define the types of roles, functions, and careers available in the field of information systems .
  5. Explain value chain with examples .
  6. Discuss pros and cons of outsourcing, offshoring, and downsizing
  7. Explain two types of innovations .
  8. Explain how soft-side implementation is a critical factor in the successful adoption of any major change .
  9. Create a database and tables in Microsoft Access.
