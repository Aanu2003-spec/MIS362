Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce concepts used to define how organizations integrate electronic and mobile commerce into their information systems to further organization goals.

After completing this module you will be able to:

  1.  Describe the current status of various forms of e-commerce, including B2B, B2C, C2C, and e-government
  2.  Outline a multistage purchasing model that describes how e-commerce works
  3.  Define m-commerce and identify some of its unique challenges
  4.  Identify several e-commerce and m-commerce applications
  5.  Identify several advantages associated with the use of e-commerce and m-commerce
  6.  Identify the many benefits and challenges associated with the continued growth of e-commerce and m-commerce
  7.  Outline the key components of a successful e-commerce and m-commerce strategy
  8.  Identify the key components of technology infrastructure that must be in place for e-commerce and m-commerce to work
  9.  Discuss the key features of the electronic payment systems needed to support e-commerce and m-commerce
